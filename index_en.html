Research

Immersive Micromanipulation System

Research on systems that enable even beginners to perform micromanipulation tasks easily, based on virtual reality and mixed reality technologies

Surface Haptic Sensations Using Wearable Devices

Research on tactile feedback in immersive environments using wearable devices composed of McKibben artificial muscles and clothing
